# Biomes Overview

The set biomes of the 40×40 km world. Each biome has distinct ecology, economy, and character.

---

## Biome List (Draft)

| Biome | Climate | Primary Resources | Village Viability |
|---|---|---|---|
| Temperate Forest | Moderate | Timber, game, berries | High |
| River Valley | Mild, fertile | Crops, fish, clay | Very high |
| Highland / Moorland | Cold, exposed | Sheep, peat, stone | Low–medium |
| Coastal | Mild, wet | Fish, salt, trade access | High (port potential) |
| Boreal / Northern | Cold, harsh | Fur, timber, isolation | Low |
| Plains / Steppe | Dry, open | Grain, horses | Medium–high |

*Biome list is a starting draft — finalize during world authoring.*

## Biome Properties
Each biome defines:
- Seasonal intensity (e.g., harsh winters in Boreal)
- Base vegetation and wildlife populations
- Resource types available
- Travel difficulty by season
