# Design Pillars

These principles arbitrate every design decision. When two ideas conflict, the pillar higher on this list wins.

---

## 1. Legacy Over Permadeath
Death is not the end of the story — it's a chapter break. Your character's children, allies, or a random stranger who inherits the consequences of your run carries the story forward. The world remembers you.

## 2. The World Lives Without You
Economy shifts, villages grow and collapse, factions war and treaty, forests regrow, wildlife migrates — all on their own simulation clocks. The player is a participant, not the protagonist of the world's story.

## 3. Scope Discipline
This is a two-person passion project. Every feature must earn its place in [[Scope|MVP]] before it earns a place in the game. Dream features are honored but parked. Shipping something real beats designing something perfect.
