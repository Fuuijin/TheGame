# NPCs

Non-player characters who populate and drive the living world.

---

## NPC Categories

### Named NPCs
- Individual characters with persistent identity, memory, and goals
- Examples: village elder, merchant, faction leader, the player's child
- Remember the player across sessions; memory fades between generations

### Generic Villagers / Citizens
- Population mass of settlements
- Drive economic production, village growth/decline
- React to conditions (famine, war, prosperity) as a group

### Merchants / Travelers
- Autonomous economic actors; drive trade simulation
- Move between settlements based on profit motive
- Can be interacted with, hired, robbed, or allied

### Soldiers / Militia
- Controlled by factions and villages
- Respond to threats, enforce borders, conduct wars

## NPC Simulation Scope
- Named NPCs: full goal/memory simulation (MVP)
- Villagers: aggregate modeling — population counters, not individuals (MVP)
- Individual soldier agents: in combat zones only (MVP)

## Integration with Legacy
- Named NPCs can become heirs or allies ([[Legacy System]])
- Heir NPCs continue to exist in world if not chosen by player
- NPC memory of prior player character decays over generations
