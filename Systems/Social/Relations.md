# Relations

How factions, villages, NPCs, and the player relate to each other — and how those relationships change.

---

## Relation Types
- **Faction ↔ Faction:** Alliance, neutral, rivalry, war
- **Village ↔ Faction:** Loyalty, tribute, occupation, independence
- **NPC ↔ Player:** Personal reputation (trust, debt, enmity)
- **Player ↔ Faction:** Standing — affects prices, quest access, safe passage

## How Relations Change
- Actions (aid, attack, trade, betray) shift standing
- Events (droughts, wars, deaths) shift faction-faction relations autonomously
- Time: old grievances fade, alliances drift without maintenance

## Legacy Interaction
- Faction standing carries across runs with a decay modifier
- Named NPCs remember the player's bloodline (V2: generational memory)
- Player can inherit enemies as well as allies

## MVP Scope
- Faction standing per faction (numeric -100 to +100)
- Personal reputation with named NPCs
- Faction-faction relations drive diplomacy simulation
