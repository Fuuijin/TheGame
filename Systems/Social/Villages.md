# Villages

The atomic unit of civilization. Settlements that live, grow, and die on their own logic.

---

## Village Lifecycle
1. **Founding** — A viable location + population seed → settlement forms
2. **Growth** — Resources, safety, and trade surplus drive population increase
3. **Stagnation** — Equilibrium; most villages spend most time here
4. **Decline** — Resource depletion, conflict, disease, or ecological collapse
5. **Abandonment / Merger** — Population disperses or joins a larger nearby settlement

## What Villages Do
- Produce goods based on biome and population
- Trade with other villages (merchant caravans)
- Recruit and maintain militia; call on faction for defense
- React to seasonal hardship (winter food stores, harvest festivals)
- Remember players and their heirs

## Village Size Tiers (MVP)
| Tier | Population | Features |
|---|---|---|
| Hamlet | < 50 | 1–2 production types, no market |
| Village | 50–500 | Market, inn, basic militia |
| Town | 500–2000 | Guild, temple, standing guard |
