# Factions

Political, cultural, or military groups that own territory, pursue goals, and conflict with each other.

---

## What a Faction Is
- A named group with territory, resources, and goals
- Can be: noble house, religious order, merchant guild, bandit confederation, ethnic/cultural group
- Has standing armies, political relationships, and economic interests

## Faction Behaviors (Simulation)
- Expand when strong, contract when weak
- Form alliances and declare wars autonomously
- React to ecological and economic pressure (famine → aggression)
- Remember player actions across runs ([[Legacy System]])

## Player Interaction
- Player can join, serve, undermine, or found factions
- Reputation with a faction carries to heirs (modified)
- Destabilizing a faction has world-level consequences

## MVP Scope
- 4–6 starting factions with fixed starting territory
- Basic diplomacy: ally, neutral, hostile
- Faction-player standing that persists
