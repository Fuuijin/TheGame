# Vegetation

Trees, crops, and ground cover as simulated resources and ecological actors.

---

## Types
- **Forests:** Hardwood, softwood, mixed — regional by biome
- **Crops:** Planted by villages/player; season- and soil-dependent
- **Grasslands / Shrubland:** Transitional, affected by overgrazing

## Key Behaviors
- Trees regrow over multi-year timescales after clearing
- Deforestation exposes soil to erosion, affects river flow (long-term)
- Crop failure possible: drought, frost, disease, soil depletion
- Player can clear, plant, manage — or neglect

## Integration
- Seasons → growth/dormancy rates
- Wildlife → grazing pressure, seed dispersal
- Villages → logging demand, farmland expansion
- Economy → timber and crop as trade goods
