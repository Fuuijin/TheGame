# Ecology Overview

The living environment: how terrain, vegetation, wildlife, and seasons interact as a self-regulating system.

---

## Subsystems
- [[Seasons & Cycles]] — Annual cycle driving all other ecology
- [[Vegetation]] — Forests, crops, regrowth, deforestation
- [[Wildlife]] — Animal populations, migration, predator/prey

## Core Principles
- Ecology runs independently of the player
- Player actions have real ecological consequences (deforestation → erosion → crop failure)
- Seasonal cycle is the primary clock for the living world

## Links to Other Systems
- Seasons → [[Economy Overview]] (harvest cycles, trade goods availability)
- Vegetation → [[Villages]] (wood supply, farmland)
- Wildlife → [[Economy Overview]] (hunting, pelts) | [[Player Character]] (food, danger)
