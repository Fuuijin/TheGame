# Economy Overview

A simulated medieval economy driven by regional supply, demand, trade routes, and population.

---

## Core Principles
- Resources are regionally scarce — no global market
- Trade routes form organically based on surplus/deficit between settlements
- Player participation affects but does not control the economy
- Economy runs between player sessions (world persistence)

## Key Systems
- **Production:** Villages produce based on biome, season, and population
- **Trade:** Caravans and merchants route between settlements autonomously
- **Prices:** Driven by local supply/demand (V2: full price simulation curves)
- **Player Impact:** Player can create trade routes, corner markets, disrupt supply

## MVP Scope
- Basic production per village type
- Merchant NPC simulation (travel, buy low/sell high)
- Price variance by region and season
